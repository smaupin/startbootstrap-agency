JITS - "Mat is Ocean"

Watercolor - Lettered and Unlettered

My homage to the classic poster for the 1975 movie Jaws, in three versions. First is a print of the ‘Title and Quote’ version of the poster. The quote is from Carlos Machado, but I have seen it attributed to various different people in different places. Because of that I felt I should do a bit of research if I was going to use it on a poster. I found a photograph of an article from Black Belt Magazine, November 1994, page 27, printing an interview with Carlos Machado, which seems to be the origin.

This is also available in both title only variations and unlettered variations. All three are available from the online shop.

"Welocme Home"

Watercolor - Lettered

I recently spent a number of months unable to do Brazilian jiu-jitsu because of a neck injury. It wasn’t fun. A few weeks ago I finally was able to go back and train jiu-jitsu, and it felt incredible just to be able to step back on the mat again after so long.

I remember that it inspired me to make some new jiu-jitsu art, which I had stopped doing while I was unable to train, so it is a ‘Welcome Home’ to me in more way than one. It's available to purchase as SD template in this portfolio sample item as a print, a poster, or several other options. The watermark is much smaller on purchased prints.

I don’t think this feeling when you get back to something you love after an absence is exclusive to BJJ or art either, I bet that people who train MMA, judo, karate, tae-kwon-do, or any TMA (Traditional Martial Arts) have similar feelings.

Stepping back on the mat feels in some way a lot like coming home. Now in the shop.
